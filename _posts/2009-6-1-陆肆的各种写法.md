罗马数字：VIIV，其实Intel专门推出的一项家庭娱乐平台就叫VIIV.  

二进制：110 100  

失去的一天：六三六五  

虚构日：五月三十五日，其实有本书真的就叫《五月三十五日》  

几何题：长六宽四周二十，列应用题形式，大约小学三、四年级题目  

扑克游戏：（5+1）（5-1），其实就是凑24，两个5，两个1  

乘方表示：八平方/四立方，特别推荐奥运游泳馆S立方  

英文：Six Four，可以简略成论坛常用词，SF  

音符：拉发，其实拉发是发廊常见业务项目之一  

谐音：柳丝/琉寺/流思，etc。其实河北高唐县确有一个琉寺镇  

农历：己巳五月初一  

生日：杨丞琳5岁生日/Angelina Jolie14岁生日/胡瓜30岁生日

Century-Fox Film Corporation), also known as 20th Century Fox, Fox 2000

Located in the Century City area of Los Angeles, just west of Beverly Hills,

by Rupert Murdoch. The company was founded in 1935, as the result of a merger

of two entities, Fox Film Corporation founded by William Fox in 1915, and

Twentieth Century Pictures, begun in 1933 by Darryl F. Zanuck, Joseph Schenck,

Raymond Griffith and William Goetz. Some of 20th Century Fox's most popular

movie franchises include the Star Wars, Home Alone, Die Hard, Ice Age, Revenge

of the Nerds, X-Men, Alien and Predator series. Some of the famous stars to

come out of this studio were Shirley Temple, Betty Grable, and Marilyn Monroe.
