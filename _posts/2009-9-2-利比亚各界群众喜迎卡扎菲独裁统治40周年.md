Gaddafi's Libya celebrates 40 Years  

September 1  

Keyword：利比亚 卡扎菲 国庆 周年 反美  

  

![](http://newsimg.bbc.co.uk/media/images/46300000/jpg/_46300229_flags2_ap.jpg)  

Libya has begun a week of celebrations to mark 40 years since the coup that

brought Colonel Muammar Gaddafi to power in the North African Country.  

  

![](http://newsimg.bbc.co.uk/media/images/46300000/jpg/_46300232_military_dancers_ap.jpg)  

Soldiers and dancers performed for African heads of state invited  to the

festivities by col Gadaffi,who's the longest-serving leader in both Africa and

the Arab world.  

  

![](http://newsimg.bbc.co.uk/media/images/46300000/jpg/_46300231_manyhorses_ap.jpg)  

Ceremonial horsemen stood on their horses as a display  of their skill at a

lavish event put on at a military field outside the capital.  

  

![](http://newsimg.bbc.co.uk/media/images/46300000/jpg/_46300230_horse_ap.jpg)  

In the other planned display over the next week handreds of dancers musicians

will chart 12,000 years of Libya's history.  

  

![](http://newsimg.bbc.co.uk/media/images/46300000/jpg/_46300228_dancers_ap.jpg)  

Once regarded as a pariah by West,Colonel Gaddafi began a return to the

international fold after Libya took responsibility for the 1988 bombing of a

Pan Am plane over the Scottish town of Lockerbie.  

  

![](http://newsimg.bbc.co.uk/media/images/46300000/jpg/_46300227_ballet_ap.jpg)  

But the hero's welcome given in Tripoli to Lockerbie bomber Abdel Ali Mohammed

al-Megrahi after his release by Scottish authorities last month has angered

many in the US and the UK.

9月1日是利比亚革命40周年纪念日，也是利比亚领导人卡扎菲执政40周年纪念日。首都黎波里为期6天的庆祝活动，让这座城市洋溢着节日气氛。到处张灯结彩，随处可见卡扎菲的巨幅画像，歌功颂德的标语更是悬挂在大街小巷。  

  

绿色广场上，还举行了声势浩大的阅兵仪式，坦克游行宣誓实力。然而，令人意想不到的是，庆典上竟然还有诸如“上吊”、“绞刑”的现场表演。别出心裁的庆祝形式，令人咋舌。  

  

值得一提的是，1988年洛克比空难的唯一罪犯迈格拉希被英国释放，前提是他必须低调回国。然而，迈格拉希却在利比亚首都机场受到了民族英雄般的热烈欢迎。这一英雄式的欢迎让卡扎菲执政40年庆典遭到西方的冷遇。
