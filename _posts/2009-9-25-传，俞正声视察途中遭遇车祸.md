Chinese delegates injured in car accident in Yalta



17:00  

Six people, including two members of the Chinese delegation and four

Ukrainians, were injured in a traffic accident in Yalta early on Thursday,

Crimea's Health Minister Serhiy Donich has told Interfax-Ukraine.  

  

He said that all of those injured were brought to Livadiya hospital.  

  

The motorcade of the Chinese delegation led by Secretary of Shanghai Municipal

Committee of China's Communist Party Yu Zhengsheng, who is on a two-day visit

to Crimea, was involved in a traffic accident on the Yalta by-pass at around

0900.  

  

The delegation was heading from Yalta to Simferopol.  

  

Those injured include China's vice consul in Ukraine who suffered a skull

fracture, and a military attache of the Chinese embassy in Ukraine whose

condition is now satisfactory.  

  

Four Ukrainians suffered various injuries and will undergo outpatient

treatment.  

  

Two Ukrainians were passengers of a VAZ-2107 car, which collided with the

motorcade of the Chinese delegation, and two other Ukrainians were

accompanying the delegation. The driver of the VAZ car was killed in the

collision.

中国副领事在雅尔塔附近的车祸中受伤 目前正在救治



22:55   2009-09-24  

  

![](http://i35.tinypic.com/xol9x4.jpg)  

  

俄新网RUSNEWS.CN辛菲罗波尔9月24日电 以上海市委书记俞正声为首的中国代表团在克里木进行为期两天的考察访问时，其车队发生交通事故。  

  

克里木交警部门新闻处向当地媒体证实，事故于24日8点至9点之间在雅尔塔附近的绕行公路发生。  

  

据克里木卫生部长谢尔盖·多尼奇称，从雅尔塔前往辛菲罗波尔的中国代表团车队当时与一辆VAZ-2107型车相撞。  

  

中国代表团的4名成员在交通事故中受伤。其中包括中国驻乌克兰副领事，他的颅骨底部骨折；而使馆一名武官的伤势被认为并不严重。  

  

消息人士称，伤者已被送往医院。中国副领事正在救治当中，他不能移动，必须留在医院。  

  

至于是否应使用急救车将使馆武官送往辛菲罗波尔，该问题仍在解决当中。  

  

VAZ-2107型轿车的司机当场死亡。车上另有两名乘客，他们也均受伤。  

  

消息人士指出，初步资料显示事故责任方为VAZ-2107型车的司机，他当时驶入了逆行车道。
