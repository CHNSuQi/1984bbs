Political paranoia of Ilija Cvorovic, 19 September 1998  

  

  

Author: Milos Eric (meric@eunet.yu) from Belgrade, Yugoslavia  

  

This Dusan Kovacevic' drama talks about political paranoia of Ilija Cvorovic,

ex-Stallinist, the man who spent two years in prison because of his activity.

He follows and haunts a tenant in his house - who returned from France. Ilija

sees in him agent of imperialist powers, enemy of the state and a foreign spy.

The movie is true piece-of-art of Yugoslav cinematography of the 80's. Also

talks about relations of hard-line communists and capitalists in liberal-

socialist Yugoslavia.
