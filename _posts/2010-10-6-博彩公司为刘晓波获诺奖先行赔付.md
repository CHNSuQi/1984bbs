博彩公司为刘晓波获诺奖先行赔付



Oct 06, 2010 (M2 PRESSWIRE via COMTEX) --  

Leading betting firm Paddy Power have today taken the unprecedented step of

paying out on imprisoned Chinese dissident Liu Xiaobo as the winner of this

years Nobel Peace Prize a full 48 hours ahead of the scheduled announcement.  

  

The bookmaker was forced to cut the odds on Liu Xiaobo winning earlier this

week from 3-1 to (odds-on) 4-5 after a late surge of betting including several

bets of 200 were placed by punters across the globe.  

  

Paddy Power spokesperson Ken Robertson explains Obviously were taking a risk

but its a calculated one. Over the past decade we have never experienced such

strong betting support for any individual candidate and this leads us to

believe that the Nobel cat is well and truly out of the bag  

  

Paddy Power are paying out over 5,000 today to all those who placed bets on

Liu Xiaobo before midnight last night.
